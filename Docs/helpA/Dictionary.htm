Dictionary

Regulations – laws, standards, requirements for a given measuring instrument. Legal regulations have the highest rank.

Assets - value under protection. Each measuring instrument has assets characteristic for its purpose, construction and functionality.

Protection property – specified in the risk estimation model as one or several features related to the asset. The set of security features in the model is constant and for measuring instruments with software includes: accessibility, integrity, unavailability, authenticity.

Threat - an unwanted event that adversely affects assets.

Attack - is a deliberate act of an aggressor who uses his potential to have a negative impact on assets. This means that there is a relationship of attack with a violation of property protection assets.

Risk - The risk is correlated with the law, the manner of implementing the attack and the aggressor's potential. Risk in this document is treated as the product of two factors:

is a function of the rank of a recipe from which assets are extracted, how the attack is implemented and the aggressor's potential. Risk - in this document is treated as the product of two factors:

ü  probability failure to comply with legal requirements or other regulations regarding the measuring instrument

ü  consequences of the violation of the above requirements (expressed by number).

The aggressor's potential includes the following factors in the model:

ü  Time needed to implement the attack,

ü  Qualifications substantive of the aggressor in the fields of knowledge required to implement the attack

ü  Knowledge which the aggressor has about the measuring device

ü  Equipment technical means necessary to implement the attack,

ü  Access to the instrument by the aggressor.
