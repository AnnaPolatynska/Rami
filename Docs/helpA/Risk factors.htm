Risk factors

Time as risk factor is presented in the range from 0 to 19. It means time represents the amount of time required by the selected attacker to implement the chosen attack. A score of 0 usually signifies work of less than a day. Required work of less than a week would give a point score of 1, whereas a score of 19 represents an estimated work period of more than half a year.

Meaning  of  scores:

ü  0 - less than 1 day,

ü  1 – 1 week or less,

ü  2 – 2 weeks or less,

ü  3 – 3 weeks or less,

ü  4 – 4 weeks or less,

ü  5, 6, 7 – 2 months or less,

ü  8, 9, 10 – 3 months or less,

ü  11, 12, 13 – 4 months or less,

ü  14, 15 – 5 months or less,

ü  18, 19 – over 6 months.

Qualifications, as risk factor is presented in the range from 0 to 8. Where 0 points represents layman capabilities and a score of  8 points are given when the attacker needs to be an expert in more than one field of science.

Meaning  of  scores:

ü  0, 1 – layman,

ü  2, 3 – proficient,

ü  4, 5, 6 – expert,

ü  7, 8 – an expert in several fields of science.

Knowledge about measuring instruments, as risk factor is presented in the range from 0 to 11. Score of 0 correspond to publicly available knowledge, such as information easily available from the websites. The maximum value (11 points) would stand for critical knowledge available only for manufacturers of the measuring equipment.

Meaning  of  scores:

ü  0 – public,

ü  1, 2, 3 – restricted,

ü  4, 5, 6, 7 – sensitive,

ü  8, 9, 10, 11 – critical, available only for manufacturers of the measuring equipment.

Equipment available for the aggressor, as risk factor is presented in the range from 0 to 9. Where 0 stand for standard equipment and score of 9 represents specialistic equipment ordered many times.

Meaning  of  scores:

ü  Standard,

ü  Specialistic,

ü  To order,

ü  Multiple order.

Access to measuring instrument, as risk factor is presented in the range from 0 to 10. If measuring instrument is connected to the Internet the access is unlimited, it means 0 points will be given in the score. Difficult access will be given the higest rank.

Meaning  of  scores:

ü  Unlimited,

ü  Easy,

ü  Standard,

ü  Difficult.
