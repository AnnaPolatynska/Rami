Risk function

 

Risk is a simple function of factors, constructed in such a way that its range is included in the values from 1 to 5. Risk (r) is calculated from the formula:

r = w * p / 5, where:

w – value influence of regulation in range from 1 to 5. If the risk arises directly from legal regulation, the impact score takes the highest value of 5. the lowest score of impact 1 takes place when the attack refers to a single measurement and can be detected by metrological supervision.

p – the probability of an attack obtained from mapping the sum of risk factors in the range from 0 to 57 to the value of probability in the range from 1 to 5.
